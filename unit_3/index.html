MAMAUSBI!

There is a lot more to musubi than just spam

Musubi can be wrapped with a thin sheet of dried seaweed (nori), covered with salted shiso leaves, or a very thin omelet called usuyaki tamago, which is like an egg crepe. It can even be served virtually naked with just a sprinkling of white or black sesame seeds. To shake things up, the rice in a musubi is sometimes mixed with

  • fresh green peas or furikake
  • a common seasoning made of dried fish
  • chopped seaweed
  • sesame seeds
